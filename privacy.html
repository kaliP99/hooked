Privacy.

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The short version

Hooked has no accounts, no server, and no analytics. Everything the app knows about you lives in your own browser and never leaves your device.

What is stored, and where

  • Your likes, playlists, followed artists, listening history, and taste profile are saved in your browser's local storage on your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of this data. We could not if we wanted to; there is no server to send it to.
  • Deleting the app data (Profile → "Delete & restart", or clearing your browser storage) erases everything permanently.
  • The optional "Export" feature saves your profile to a file on your device. That file is yours; share it only with people you trust.

Third parties

  • Apple (iTunes Search API): when you use the app, your browser requests song metadata and 30-second previews directly from Apple. Apple sees these requests the same way it sees any visit to its services. See Apple's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts: the app loads its typeface from Google's CDN, which sees a standard web request from your browser.
  • Links to YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Music open those services; their own policies apply there.

Cookies

Hooked sets no cookies and runs no trackers or analytics of any kind.

Changes

If Hooked ever adds accounts or any data collection, this page will describe exactly what changes before it happens.
